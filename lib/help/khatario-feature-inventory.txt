Khatario — in-app feature inventory (from main navigation, Settings, and related routes)
Generated for help-centre planning. Some items are plan-gated (Upgrade) or require settings (e.g. warehouses).

DASHBOARD
- Dashboard (home), cash flow card, other widgets as enabled

SUPPLIER MODE (if your business is linked as a supplier to customers)
- Supplier dashboard
- Requests to fulfill

SALES
- Customers (masters, credit, statements)
- Invoices
- Quotations (estimates)
- Sales orders
- Delivery challans
- Work orders
- Credit notes
- Debit notes

PURCHASES
- Suppliers
- Suppliers hub
- All purchases
- Purchase requests
- Purchase orders
- Purchase returns
- Expenses (categories, on-account, paid, etc.)

INVENTORY
- Items (products/services)
- Print labels (barcode / plan feature)
- Warehouses (when enabled; multi-warehouse may be plan-gated)
- Stock transfers (with warehouses)
- Inventory adjustments
- Stock summary, closing stock (reports)

ACCOUNTING
- Chart of accounts
- Journal entries (incl. templates)
- General ledger
- Payments in (from customers)
- Payments out (to suppliers, etc.)
- Provisions
- TDS / TCS (categories, payments, certificates, transactions)
- Opening balances (setup)

REPORTS (overview page + builder)
- Custom report builder (plan)
- Profit & loss
- Balance sheet
- Cash flow (statement)
- Trial balance
- Receivables aging, payables aging
- Sales: summary, invoice-wise, item-wise, party-wise, tax-wise, payment mode, discount, credit sales, cancelled, returns, B2B vs B2C
- Purchase: summary, invoice-wise, supplier-wise, returns, credit, tax-wise
- GST: GSTR-1, GSTR-2B, GSTR-2B reconciliation, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9, label print log
- Inter-branch reconciliation (if applicable)
- Stock valuation, label print log (when enabled)

HR & EMPLOYEES (plan / capability gated)
- Employees, add employee
- Attendance (mark, etc.)
- Leaves
- Salary payments, advances, payslips
- Commissions
- Performance, tasks
- Activity logs
- Settings: commission rules, holidays, leave types, shifts

MORE — TOOLS
- To-do list, HSN/SAC finder, GST calculator, TDS calculator, discount/price/EMI/interest, PAN/GSTIN validators, lead extractor, image tools, etc.
- All tools index

MORE — WHATSAPP (add-on)
- Dashboard, conversations, order verification, bot rules, send message, campaigns, contacts, groups, group extractor, unsubscribes
- Settings: WhatsApp integration

SETTINGS (organization)
- Organization, suppliers directory, financial years, branches, warehouses, user management, users, roles, user branches/warehouses, activity
- Tax & GST, GST configuration
- Account mappings, period locks
- Plan & subscription
- UI features, backup & restore
- Templates & printing, invoice design, label templates, Bluetooth printer, number series, digital signature
- Number series, holidays, leave types, shifts, commission rules
- Integrations (WhatsApp, HR, SMS, AI, CRM, etc.)
- Help: product tour, how-to guides, documentation links

OTHER APP AREAS
- Search
- Credit approvals (if used)
- Notifications
- Provisions, debit/credit note flows, purchase/sales order flows
- Admin console (separate app area for platform operators): businesses, plans, HSN, policies, etc.

This list is not a commitment that every sub-screen has a dedicated user guide; guides are written for the most common workflows.
